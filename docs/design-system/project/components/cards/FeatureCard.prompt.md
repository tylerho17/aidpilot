# FeatureCard

Marketing / dashboard feature tile: a tinted `IconTile`, title, and a line of copy on the lifting white card. Optional corner `badge` (e.g. "Coming soon").

```jsx
<FeatureCard icon="grid" tone="blue" title="Aid dashboard">
  See if your aid is protected, what's due soon, and what could put money at risk.
</FeatureCard>
<FeatureCard icon="letter" tone="amber" title="Aid letter decoder" badge="Coming soon">
  Turn confusing grants, loans, and work-study into a simple explanation.
</FeatureCard>
```
